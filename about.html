Why did I choose Applied computer science at Thomas More

Studying in a bilingual high school gave me the opportunity the look abroad for the option to continue my education. I applied to several universities around Europe but at the end, course Applied computer science at Thomas more became the best option. The course suited my interests and was financially viable.
