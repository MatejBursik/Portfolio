ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Matej Buršík and I am a programmer and a problem solver. I am currently studying Applied Computer Science at Thomas More. I am interested Application development and Data science. My preferred style of tasks are those which require creative problem solving and are of medium size. In my free time, I enjoy doing sports and working on my person projects. I was born in Slovakia and decided to explore abroad for learning and job opportunities.

Why did I choose Applied computer science at Thomas More

Studying in a bilingual high school gave me the opportunity the look abroad for the option to continue my education. I applied to several universities around Europe but at the end, course Applied computer science at Thomas more became the best option. The course suited my interests and was financially viable.

Future dreams and ambitions

In the future, I would like to find application development or data science related job. Since those are my fields of interests, most of my projects lean it those directions. Yearly, I participate in Advent of Code. It is a programming competition hosted from 1st till 25th of December targeted towards data science and problem solving. They daily publish a task with two parts which result in 50 points. Last few years I managed to reach around 30 points and in future I would like to reach all 50 points. I enjoy working on these tasks because they challenge me creatively and analytically to find a suitable approach for the tasks. I would also like to expand my portfolio with some new projects. One of the projects that I am working on is making and publishing a video game. Another project is something to display my understanding of Java in terms of web applications. In the future, I would also like to travel a bit more around Europe and Asia to experience new cultures. Lastly, I would like to improve at some sports that I am doing like surfing and climb.
